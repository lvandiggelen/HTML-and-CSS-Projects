About

Hello, my name is DJ Lewis. I want to make your next event, One To Remember!
I do not scratch. I just play the best music tailored to your event. My passion for music developed at the end of High School when I attended my first General Admission concert and I got to see Swedish House Mafia on their last tour. I walked into the event teeming with excitement. I walked out sold to the love of music. Through college I grew this love by becoming a DJ.
My roommates and I had a lot of parties and before I started mixing, and we would have our DJ friend Quentin perform. The only problem was that he would always play heavy electronic music that did not appeal to most of the crowd. There came a point when we needed a change, so I stepped to the plate. Before long I was behind the turntables playing the songs everyone was asking for. The one thing I have enjoyed most about being a DJ is reading the audience and catering the music to what they desire. I am constantly asked, “What music do you play at your events?” and my consistent answer is, “I play what the crowd wants to hear.” I started in 2015, and since then I have grown my skills and expanded my setup. My first gigs were at friends’ parties, but I quickly expanded to playing for Fraternity date nights, college events, and downtown clubs. Since college I have played at many weddings, Bar & Bat Mitzvahs, Birthday parties, and School Dances. For two years I was the resident DJ for Almaden Country School. I always looked forward to playing exactly what excited those middle school kids most. If you would like, I could connect you with previous clients so you can hear first hand how fun my events are.
I am a young, jubilant DJ bursting with energy that I would love to bring to your next event. I am equipped for any situation. If you have any questions about me or my rates please feel free to call at (408) 931-0531 or send me an email at Lvandiggelen@gmail.com.
